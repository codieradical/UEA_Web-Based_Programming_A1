Local Attractions

There's plently of great activities you can enjoy during your visit; as well as many to places to eat, both for a fancy sit-down meal or an easy take-out. Our hotel may be surrounded by nature meaning there aren't many close-by services, but theres still plenty to do that's only a short drive away!

Take the time to explore the lovely, scenic area around our hotel; or go golfing on a relaxing Sunday morning. Perhaps you want to relax at a professional quality Spa? These and more surround the you'll be staying in.

Listed below are several different leisure and dining services that can be found nearby. Additionally we have provided a map so you may expore the area yourself and plan your stay before you arrive.

Leisure and activities

Sunny Hills: Golf course

Sunny Hills: Golf course is a full scale golf course where you can chill out and play games of golf. The course is located 20 minutes West of the hotel, or 30 minutes vis the bus.

Play a relaxing game of golf on a course of 16 challenging holes. Then chill and get a hot or cold drink at the Sunny Hills cafe afterwards. With lots of available outside seating areas and a wide selection of drinks and pastries, it is a day out we strongly recommend.

Games should be booked a few weeks in advance. Eqipment can be rented on-site, including clubs, balls, and carts to travel between courses on. For inquiries call 0460 418 564.

Body and Mind: Spa

The Body and Mind: Spa is a great place to relax and get rid of built up stress. Renown for their wide range of therapeutic treatments, it is certainly a location rated highly by out guests.

Offering services ranging from massages, to saunas, to manicures and pedicures, theres something for everyone. It's the perfect place to leave all your stress behind.

Bookings may be required when planning to visit at peak times. We would recommend inquiring before visiting to avoid being turned away. Inquiries and pricing information avaiable on their website, or by calling 0460 267 674.

Dining

Garmonbozia

Garmonbozia is a traditional style, 5 star restaurant located 15 minutes away from the hotel by car, 20 minutes by bus, or 50 minutes by foot.

Enjoy fine dining with world class service and dishes. Some of the food served has won awards and attracted famous names to the restaurant.

Tables must be booked in advance via their website or by calling 04680 432 231. We advice booking up to a week in advance due to popular demand shown in recent surveys.

Double R Diner

The Double R Diner is a classic american style diner, located 20 minutes away from the hotel by car, 30 minutes by bus, and 1 hour by foot.

Themed like a traditional American diner, enjoy friendly customer that always have a smile on their faces. The decoration and commitment of the staff really bring the place together.

Our personal recommedations for a visit would be to get the 'All-American burger', which comes with a side of fries. We would also recommned a classic, thick American milkshake (made with ice cream), and the diner's signature cherry pie for dessert.

No table booking required. To inquire about prices and other queries contact them on 0460 456 267.
